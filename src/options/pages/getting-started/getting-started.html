Welcome

Your First Moves

Welcome to your interactive chess companion. Chess Buzz processes active web-based boards to deliver instantaneous tactical feedback, and when authorized, enacts sequences autonomously. This manual serves as your orientation.

Initialization

Summon the interface by interacting with the toolbar icon. The extension immediately scans the visible window for a chessboard and begins evaluation. However, optimal usage implies keeping the interface readily accessible.

1
Docking the Tool

To assure immediate retrieval, affix the icon directly to your application header. Click the puzzle icon adjacent to your URL bar, locate the "Chess Buzz" listing, and select the pin emblem.

Sustaining the Window

By default, extension panels close when focus shifts. To force the interface to remain omnipresent during a session, right-click the pinned emblem and choose 'Inspect Popup'.

Observation: This action tethers the panel, keeping it active so long as the developer pane remains unfettered.

Deep Dive

A specialized button resides near the edge of the interface. When struck, it instantly ports the ongoing match setup into a robust Lichess study tab, accommodating extensive theoretical probing.
