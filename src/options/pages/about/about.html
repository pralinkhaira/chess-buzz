Purpose

The Origins

Chess Buzz started as a personal improvement project inspired by the work of Alex Petrusca. I came across his repository and really appreciated the idea and implementation. However, the project appeared mostly inactive, and I wanted to make some changes tailored to my own workflow and usability preferences.

Initial Goals
  • Improve the UI for better clarity and comfort
  • Refine certain interactions for smoother gameplay
  • Fix minor bugs and edge-case issues I encountered

As I worked on it, the project gradually evolved beyond just small tweaks. I redesigned parts of the interface to feel more modern and minimal, optimized performance in certain areas, improved responsiveness, and cleaned up parts of the codebase for maintainability.

Evolution

A Customized Iteration

Chess Buzz is not just a copy or mirror. It is a customized and enhanced iteration built with my own design choices, structural adjustments, and improvements focused on usability and stability.

This project is being released publicly to:

  • Provide a cleaner, more modern user experience
  • Maintain and actively improve features that users rely on
  • Encourage open collaboration and transparency
  • Experiment with UI/UX enhancements and performance optimization

Full credit and respect go to the original concept and implementation that inspired this work. Chess Buzz represents my own take on improving and extending that idea.

Please star the repo and follow me!
